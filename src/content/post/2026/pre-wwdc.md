Yep, that time of year has arrived. Most Apple developers either gather together near the mothership or remotely in anticipation of the conference and what we will get this year.

Naturally there are always the obvious things, new major platforms, tools, and frameworks that will be announced along with OS releases. Plus the continued hope that Xcode will continue to evolve and improve.

But often it's the things you don't hear about in the keynote of State of the Union that make the real news in the developer community.

For me, I'll be watching and with everyone and I'll be appearing in Podcasts, which is something I look forward to doing every year with my developer friends.

So while I gather my strength today and clean off my plate of the little things that I need to get done before Monday, I recommend keeping an eye on this blog and my Podcasts.
